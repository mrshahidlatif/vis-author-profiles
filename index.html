VIS Author Profiles

VIS Author Profiles (VAP) describes publication records of authors from the visualization community in the form of interactive reports that combine automatically generated text and visualizations. The profiles are based on data from DBLP, the Visualization Publication Data Collection, and KeyVis. VAP uses a subset of the DBLP dataset, containing 127,871 publications until 16 July 2018. And our system produce profile pages of 5,087 authors working in the visualization community. VAP is a prototype and might contain occassional bugs or performance issues. If you encounter any problem or have a suggestion, please feel free to contact us.

Ready to explore?
Type an author's name in the search box or start with a random author's profile. Every author has an individual profile page.

DISCLAIMER: The author's profile text is automatically generated, based on the analysis of bibliographic metadata. Therefore, we assume no responibility or liability for any inaccuracies or incompleteness in the contents of a profile page.
